Some climate scientists have created a machine learning model that can predict how much carbon can be reduced by implementing policies. Their model has 3 inputs.

  • Banning single-use plastics
  • Driving less
  • Eating less meat

The scientists' model shows what the reduction in carbon would be with different combinations of policies. Active policies are shown in blue.

Even though it would make most sense to implement all three policies, the government only wants to start with one. They have asked the scientists which of the policies is most impactful. Because the scientists don't know, they've turned to you to find out for them.

Using the data from the scientists' model, can you tell them which policy leads to the biggest reduction in carbon?
